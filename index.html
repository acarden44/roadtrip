Where to Stay & Eat

Falling Rock Café

A cozy Munising café known for its homemade treats, live music, and local bookstore vibes.

Address: 104 E Munising Ave

What I like about it: The best place to chill with coffee after a waterfall hike.

Dapple-Gray B&B

Nestled in Eagle Harbor, this family-run bed & breakfast offers charming rooms and lake views.

Address: 651 Gratiot St

What I like about it: Feels like staying at your aunt’s rustic lake house — but fancier.

Fitzgerald's Restaurant

Smoked brisket, Lake Superior views, and legendary bourbon selection in Eagle River.

Address: 5033 Front St

What I like about it: The view. The ribs. The vibe. Just go.

More About the U.P.

The Upper Peninsula of Michigan is rich with history, waterfalls, lighthouses, and natural beauty. It's a haven for hikers, photographers, and anyone craving a rugged escape.
